Data and scoring method

The static demo uses real PTV metro train, tram and bus stop extracts, Victorian school locations, AIHW hospital points, sport facility points and LGA profile indicators bundled from the prior cloud analytics project. Property candidates are manually supplied examples, not scraped listings.

Limitations

Distances are straight-line approximations. Scores are simplified decision-support indicators and do not replace property, transport or planning advice. Retail anchors are curated demonstration points; a production refresh should query OSMnx or Overpass and optionally use routing APIs.

Next steps

Add OSMnx amenity refresh, optional OpenRouteService travel-time matrices, Streamlit CSV upload, and MLflow tracking for score-weight experiments.
